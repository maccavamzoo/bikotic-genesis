# Specialized Tarmac SL8 vs Trek Madone Gen 7

The ultimate aero vs lightweight race bike showdown. We compare geometry, aerodynamics, and real-world performance.

## Geometry Comparison

The Tarmac SL8 features a more aggressive racing geometry:

- Stack: 563mm
- Reach: 398mm
- Head tube angle: 73.5°
- Weight: 6.8kg (claimed)

The Madone Gen 7 prioritizes aerodynamics:

- Stack: 571mm
- Reach: 395mm
- Head tube angle: 72.5°
- Weight: 7.1kg (claimed)

## Key Differences

**Tarmac SL8:**
- Lighter by 230g
- Steeper geometry (sharper handling)
- Better for climbing
- More responsive in corners

**Madone Gen 7:**
- More aerodynamic profile
- IsoFlow comfort technology
- Better for flat/rolling terrain
- Faster on straightaways

## Conclusion

For crit racing and hilly courses: **Tarmac SL8**

For time trials and flat road races: **Madone Gen 7**
